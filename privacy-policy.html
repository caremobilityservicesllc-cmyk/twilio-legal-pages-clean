Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

Care Mobility Services LLC uses SMS and related communication tools to support non-emergency medical transportation operations, including trip confirmations, reminders, dispatch updates, and driver arrival notices.

Information We Collect

  • Message delivery records and response history

How We Use Information

  • To send operational updates and reminders

SMS Consent and Data Sharing

SMS consent is not shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing purposes. We do not sell personal information.

Data Protection

We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards to protect the information we process.

Your Choices

You may reply STOP to opt out of SMS messages. You may reply HELP for support.

Contact
